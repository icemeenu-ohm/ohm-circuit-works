Services

Hand Soldering

IPC-A-610 & J-STD-001 based soldering for through-hole, selective rework, connector soldering, and repairs. Fine-pitch work and conformal coating prep available.

Wire Harness & Cable Assembly

Cutting, stripping, crimping, terminal application, heat-shrink tubing, harness routing, and continuity testing. Standards: IPC/WHMA-A-620.

Electro-Mechanical Assembly

PCB installation, connector panel assembly, enclosure hardware installation, and torque-controlled fastenings with verification records.

Inspection & Testing

Incoming inspection, in-process checks, final visual inspection to IPC-A-610 class 2 (or customer-specified). Continuity and functional testing available.

Note: We follow IPC-A-610, IPC/WHMA-A-620, and J-STD-001 workmanship standards and align our processes with ISO 9001:2015 principles. We are not ISO certified at this time.
