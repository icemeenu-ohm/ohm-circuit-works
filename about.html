About Ohm Circuit Works

Founded to bring professional-grade assembly processes to startups, R&D labs, and small manufacturers, Ohm Circuit Works focuses on quality, communication, and flexibility. With over 45+ combined years of experience in PCB manufacturing, process engineering, DFM, quality, and electronic assembly, we provide reliable workmanship for prototypes and small-batch production.

Our Commitments

  • Adhere to IPC-A-610, J-STD-001, and IPC/WHMA-A-620 workmanship standards.
  • Implement ISO 9001:2015 principles across our QMS (preparation stage).
  • Maintain traceability and transparent records for every job.

Who We Serve

Startups, contract engineers, product developers, educational institutions, and small-scale manufacturers requiring high-quality, low-volume assembly.
